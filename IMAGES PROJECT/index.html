Types of Images

The first type of image is the GIF. GIFs are a good resource for simple graphics because of their low file size. However, the downside being that they are restricted to 256 colors. This is not eniugh to make a image look like a real lifr scene.

The next type of iamge is the JPG. JPG are a artists best bet when working with photographs contaning complex images.

The last of type of image is the PNG. which is good because it gives you the most amount of availble colors.
